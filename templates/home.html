About Me

I've been interested in training strategies and the science behind them for years now. I wanted to find a way to achieve a desirable level of muscle and fitness, but without having it take over my life. Sure, I could work out like The Rock, spending hours at the gym and eating thousands of calories worth of chicken and rice each day, but this isn't desirable. I wanted it to look effortless. I wanted to fit fitness into my lifestyle, not have my lifestyle revolve around fitness.


I've spent countless hours pouring over fitness and nutrition articles. The problem is, there are so many opinions out there, I struggled to find something that worked for me. If you're like me, you've looked up workout tips and strategies online and tried a whole lot of them yourself without many, if any, results. And that's frustrating.


I feel as though I've finally narrowed down the strategies that really work. I've taken them all and compiled them here into a one-stop, end-all guide to achieving your health and fitness goals. I wanted to take all that research and training I did, bottle it up and provide it to the masses in one concise guide.



Hope you enjoy, and I can't wait to hear the success stories.

-Chris Levine
